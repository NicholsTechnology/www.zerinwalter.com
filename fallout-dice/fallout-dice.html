Welcome to Fallout Dice, this is a game based off of D&D, Fallout New Vegas, & Fallout 4. Fallout dice is a game where you make your own character, and use it to play with your friends, so basically modded D&D. It is very simple if you know how to play Fallout & D&D. If you do not know how to play some of those, we will provide a guide to Fallout Dice on this website sometime in 2025. Thank you for visiting this website, have a good day! -Zerin Nichols, co founder of Fallout Dice
